Michael Jordan

The man who changed sport

Life time line:

  • 1963 - Born in Brooklyn, New York.
  • 1981 - Gets a scholarship from North Carolina.
  • 1984 - Leaves the University to enter the NBA Draft.
  • 1984 - Gets selected by the Chicago Bulls.
  • 1985 - Wins NBA Rookie of the year and the Air Jordan I's get released by Nike.
  • 1991 - Gets nominated finals Most Valuable Player (MVP) as the bulls defeat the Los Angeles Lakers, to win their first title.
  • 1992 - Gets nominated finals MVP and wins its second consecutive NBA title.
  • 1993 - Gets nominated for the third consecutive time finals MVP and the Bulls become the first franchise in 27 years to win three consecutive titles.
  • 1993 - Stuns the basketball world by retiring but this is short-lived.
  • 1996 - Wins another time the finals MVP and Space Jam gets released.
  • 1997 - Gets nominated for the fifth time finals MVP, becoming the player with most MVPs of all time.
  • 1998 - Wins for the sixth time finals MVP.
  • 2003 - Announces his retirement from the NBA.
